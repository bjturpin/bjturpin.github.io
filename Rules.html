House Rules

Obviously, no in-game exploits. Out-of-game exploits (E.g. mailing me anthrax) are fine.

No breaking character in Discord during sessions. You're a 15th Century Diplomat and you'll like it.

Ledger is limited. Difficulty is set to Normal. New World is standard.

Sessions hosted on Tuesdays from 7PM-10PM.

Eliminated Players should expect to be released in treaties.

All players should anticipate constant land gain and loss.

Underhanded sleights are highly encouraged.

Winner gets a DLC or a weekend get-away to Richmond, Virginia.
